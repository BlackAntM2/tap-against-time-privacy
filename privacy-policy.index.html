Introduction

Tap Against Time ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Progress Data: We store your game statistics locally on your device, including:
    • High scores
    • Level progress
    • Achievement unlocks
    • Gameplay statistics
    • Settings preferences
    This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Our third-party advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and advertising data for the purpose of serving personalized ads.
  • Usage Data: AdMob may collect information about how you interact with advertisements in the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To display advertisements (through Google AdMob)
  • To process in-app purchases (handled by Apple through StoreKit)
  • To maintain game statistics and achievements locally on your device

Third-Party Services

Tap Against Time uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Displaying advertisements
  • Data Collected: Device identifiers, advertising data
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

Apple StoreKit

  • Purpose: Processing in-app purchases
  • Data Collected: Purchase information (handled by Apple)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Data Storage and Security

  • All game progress and statistics are stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate servers that store your personal game data
  • Third-party services (AdMob) have their own security measures in place
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data we handle

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With advertising partners (Google AdMob) for the purpose of serving ads
  • As required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Tap Against Time is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored game data (through the app's Statistics screen)
  • Delete your game data (by deleting and reinstalling the app)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (through your device settings)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Consent
